Our Story

Built on a Belief

Every child has the potential to be an innovator. Youngovator was founded to give them the tools, environment, and inspiration to build the future.

Mr. Mudit Thakkar
Robot Man of India

Mr. Mudit Thakkar

Founder & CEO, Youngovator | Robot Man of India

A passionate STEM educator and entrepreneur from Indore, Mudit founded Youngovator in 2019 with a single mission: to bring world-class robotics and technology education to every child in India — regardless of background or geography.

Popularly known as the "Robot Man of India", Mudit has personally trained over 5,000 students and built partnerships with 50+ schools across Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Delhi. His innovative teaching methods have been recognized by IIT Ropar and national STEM bodies.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the lab setup take?+
From site visit to launch, the complete setup typically takes 15–30 days. We coordinate everything including procurement, installation, network setup, and teacher training. You just provide the space!
Do we need to hire separate STEM teachers?+
No. Youngovator provides trained instructors as part of the Standard and Premium packages. We can also train your existing staff through our 3-day Teacher Training Program included with all packages.
What is included in the curriculum?+
Our curriculum covers Robotics (LEGO → Arduino → Raspberry Pi), 3D Design & Printing, Coding (Scratch → Python), IoT, and Electronics. It's age-differentiated for grades 3–12, CBSE/ICSE/State Board aligned, and includes project-based assessments.
What happens if equipment breaks?+
All packages include 1-year hardware warranty. Our team handles repairs and replacements within 48 hours. Annual maintenance contracts are available for ongoing coverage.
Can we customise the lab for our school's space?+
Absolutely. Our designers create a completely custom lab layout for your exact room dimensions, existing infrastructure, and student capacity. The site visit is specifically for this assessment.
Do you help with competition preparation?+
Yes! We have a dedicated competition coaching team for WRO, WSRO, CODEAVOUR, and iCode Hackathon. Standard and Premium labs get included competition prep. Basic labs can add this as an optional service.
